\section{Conclusion}

A KG-centered design makes construction takeoff and cut optimization \emph{auditable, improvable, and reusable}.
By combining structured rules and historical precedents with agentic reasoning and deterministic optimization,
Construction.AI can generate practical BOMs, waste-minimizing cut lists, and build instructions that connect directly to
supplier workflows.

The system builds on a solid foundation of existing parsing and extraction capabilities,
adding the knowledge graph layer that enables grounded reasoning and continuous improvement from real-world outcomes.
